About USWA Public School

USWA Public School is an English-medium institution established under the Rahmat-ul-lil-Aalameen Educational & Charitable Trust (R), located Near Govt. Urdu School, ABL Housing Society, Shantnagar, BHANKUR, Shahabad – 585228.

Our institution provides a comprehensive educational experience that combines academic excellence with moral and ethical development. We believe in nurturing young minds to become responsible citizens who contribute positively to society.

From Pre-Nursery to Class 10, USWA offers a supportive learning environment that respects every student's unique potential and fosters their overall development.
